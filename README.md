## 🌟 Overview

**DiabetCare** is a revolutionary health-tech platform that combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with holistic wellness approaches to help individuals assess, prevent, and manage diabetes risk. Unlike traditional health apps that require expensive lab tests, DiabetCare offers an innovative **Quick Check** feature using only basic information you already know.

### 🎯 The Problem We Solve

Traditional diabetes risk assessment requires:
- 💉 Blood tests (expensive, time-consuming)
- 🏥 Doctor visits (not always accessible)
- 📊 Multiple lab parameters (hard to obtain)
- ⏰ Waiting for results (creates anxiety)

**Our Solution:** Smart risk assessment using **Weight, Height & Age** - information everyone knows! Optional glucose reading if you have a home glucometer.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🚀 Core Assessment Tools

#### ⚡ Quick Check (No Lab Tests Required!)
- **Input Required:** Weight, Height, Age
- **Optional:** Home glucometer reading
- **Output:** AI-powered risk assessment in seconds
- **Why it Works:** BMI (calculated from weight/height) is one of the strongest diabetes predictors
- **Perfect For:** Initial screening, regular monitoring, anyone without recent lab results

#### 🔬 Full Assessment (With Lab Results)
- Complete analysis using 8 medical parameters
- Detailed risk breakdown with probability scores
- Comprehensive health recommendations
- Downloadable PDF reports

## 📊 How It Works

### Quick Check Algorithm

### Evidence-Based Approach

**Why BMI Works:**
- 📚 Proven correlation with diabetes risk (Studies: Mokdad et al., 2003)
- 🔬 Strong predictor in multiple populations (WHO Guidelines)
- 📊 Easy to calculate, universally applicable
- ✅ Validated by American Diabetes Association

**Clinical Accuracy:**
- BMI ≥30: 7x increased diabetes risk
- BMI ≥35: 20x increased diabetes risk
- Age ≥45: 2x increased baseline risk

## 📚 Dataset Information

### Pima Indians Diabetes Database

**Source:** National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases (NIDDK)

**Specifications:**
- 📊 768 patient medical records
- 🔬 8 medical predictor variables
- 🎯 Binary outcome classification
- 👥 Female patients aged 21+ of Pima Indian heritage
- ✅ Peer-reviewed and widely used in medical research

**Variables:**
1. Pregnancies - Number of pregnancies
2. Glucose - Plasma glucose concentration
3. Blood Pressure - Diastolic blood pressure (mm Hg)
4. Skin Thickness - Triceps skin fold thickness (mm)
5. Insulin - 2-Hour serum insulin (mu U/ml)
6. BMI - Body mass index (weight/height²)
7. Diabetes Pedigree Function - Family history factor
8. Age - Age in years
